Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use the LifeLedger app ("the App"), including:

  • Account information (such as email address and profile details).
  • Financial data (such as expenses and budgets you input).
  • Personal diary entries, photos, and memories you upload.
  • Device information and usage data automatically collected for analytics and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's features (Expense Tracking, Diary, Memories, Reminders).
  • Sync your data across your devices securely.
  • Send technical notices, updates, and support messages.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no internet transmission is ever completely secure or error-free.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted third-party services that assist us in operating our app (e.g., cloud hosting providers, analytics services) under strict confidentiality agreements. We may also disclose information if required by law.

5. Your Choices

You can review, modify, or delete your data directly within the App's settings. If you wish to delete your account entirely, please contact us at support@lifeledger.example.com.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@lifeledger.example.com
